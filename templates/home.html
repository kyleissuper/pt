What is this web app?

This is a (very buggy!) hands on thought experiment about how analytical infrastracture could be valuable from a customer service manager's perspective, hacked together over two days.

We begin with a hypothetical dataset that I've hosted publicly on Google Sheets.

Some key features include:

  • Integration: the data visualized comes from Google Sheets, and could easily be replaced with another dataset so long as it follows the same object/property/relationship model.
  • Drill down analysis: click on any node in the graph to see its properties, and click on property values to update the graph.
  • Sharing: click the "Share this analysis" link to copy the current graph's URL to your clipboard.

Possible example insights:

  • Noberto really wants help upgrading their account!
  • Customers representing large companies may benefit from more proactive product training.
  • Indeed, tickets with the title "How to do this?" tend to be large customers (large customer nodes are colored black).
